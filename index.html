About Me

Greetings, I'm Alexander T. Bagual, a recent graduate of Cavite State University CCAT Campus with a degree in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I bring a versatile creative flair and a deep passion for transforming ideas into visually engaging experiences.

During my academic journey, I had the opportunity to troubleshoot Desktops, Laptops, and Printers. Additionally, my hands-on experience includes the development of three expiration date programs in various projects during my On-the-Job Training at Semitec Electronics Philippines, Inc.

My skill set encompasses expertise in Graphic Designing and Video Editing, advanced proficiency in Web Designing, and adept usage of office applications such as PowerPoint, Excel, and Word. I exhibit proficiency in troubleshooting both hardware and software issues and possess intermediate skills in VBA Programming. While I'm a beginner in Javascript, Python, and C++, I'm eager to explore and expand my programming skills.

Embark on this creative journey with me, where innovation meets dedication.
