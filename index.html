About Me

Hello, my name is Alexander T. Bagual, and I am a dedicated IT professional with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Cavite State University CCAT Campus. I have six months of professional experience as an Graphic Designer at PEP-E Marketing and Services in Rosario, Cavite, where I have refined my skills across various creative disciplines and contributed significantly to numerous IT-related jobs within the company.

In addition to my full-time position, I have also engaged in freelance graphic design projects, collaborating with both local and international clients. My expertise includes visual merchandising, posters, creative manipulation, digital art, and logo design. I am committed to innovation and precision, consistently delivering solutions that enhance user experience and engage diverse audiences.
